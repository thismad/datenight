Day 01 · Monday, July 28

The Rendezvous

two flights, one port

15:00
Hydrofoil, Naples → Ischia

I'll be at the port. This is the part where we meet: no pressure, just a boat, the bay of Naples, and 50 minutes to get used to each other's faces.

A Fiat convertible is waiting. Fifteen minutes of hairpins and bougainvillea to a place I'm not naming. You'll get the front door and nothing else.

17:30
Check-in

I upgraded a few things about the room. You'll see when we open the door.

Golden hour
Aperitivo at the hotel

Nowhere to be. The gardens, a drink, the day cooling down around us.

Evening
First dinner, at the hotel

Day one is for arriving. We stay in, eat well, and take things slow: let's at least try to keep security out of it on our first night.

The boring details
  • Your flight: Ryanair FR4090, BUD 11:10 → NAP 12:50, seat 19A, priority + 2 cabin bags
  • Airport → port: Alibus or taxi, ~30 min. I'll be at Porto di Beverello.
  • Hydrofoil from Napoli Porto di Beverello, dep. 15:00 (~50 min)
  • Hotel: classified. Three nights. Trust me.
Day 02 · Tuesday, July 29

The Thermal Day

warm water, zero decisions
Slow a.m.
Breakfast in the gardens

No alarm. The island isn't going anywhere.

Midday
Poseidon thermal gardens

Twenty-something pools terraced above the sea, each a different temperature. We work our way up. Or down. There is no wrong order.

Golden hour
Belvedere above Sant'Angelo

The viewpoint from that photo on the first page: I took it standing exactly where we'll be standing, spritz in hand.

Evening
Dinner in Sant'Angelo

Car-free village, seafood, slow evenings. We walk down, we eat too much, we take our time.

The boring details
  • Giardini Poseidon Terme, Citara bay: full-day tickets, towels included
  • Sant'Angelo: ~20 min by car from the hotel, park above, walk down

top down, music up
Morning
The full loop, by convertible

Ischia is 34 km around. We do all of them: you own the playlist, I own the hairpins.

Midday
Swim at San Montano

A shallow, warm, ridiculous bay. We stay until we're hungry.

Afternoon
Ischia Ponte & Castello Aragonese

The castle on its own rock, connected by a stone bridge. We cross it, climb it, and pretend we're scouting wedding venues as a joke. As a joke.

Evening
Dinner: classified

The good table, saved for the last full night. You'll know when we're there.

The boring details

  • San Montano bay: arrive before 11 for a good spot
  • Dinner: nice try. Table booked, that's all you get.
